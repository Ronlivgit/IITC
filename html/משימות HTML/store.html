Renuar

Renuar (Hebrew: רנואר) is a fashion retailer in Israel that operates the fashion chain of the "Renuar" and "Twentyfourseven" brands. This is the fifth largest fashion chain in Israel. The chain markets clothing, footwear, jewelry, bags, and fashion accessories.
